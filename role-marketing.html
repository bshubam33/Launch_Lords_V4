Digital Marketing Strategist

Location: Remote Type: Full-Time

Overview

Launch Lords is hiring a Digital Marketing Strategist to help build awareness and demand for our AI automation systems. This role focuses on developing marketing initiatives that position the company as a leader in AI-driven business infrastructure.

You will work on content distribution, campaign strategy, and growth initiatives across multiple channels.

Responsibilities

  • Plan and execute digital marketing campaigns
  • Develop positioning and messaging strategies
  • Manage social media and content distribution
  • Analyze campaign performance and optimize strategy
  • Coordinate with content and growth teams
  • Identify new channels for audience acquisition

Requirements

  • Experience in digital marketing or growth roles
  • Strong understanding of social media and content marketing
  • Ability to analyze marketing data and improve campaigns
  • Strong communication and strategic thinking skills
  • Familiarity with modern marketing tools and platforms
